# How to buy a product?

{% hint style="danger" %}
THIS IS _DEPRECATED_ **SINCE** THE **TRANSITION TO KEYMASTER**
{% endhint %}

**Thank you for your interest** in one or more of our products. We know that our system is unique and may not seem immediately understandable to some. For this this tutorial, here is explained step by step how you can easily complete the checkout successfully.

{% hint style="info" %}
Please make sure that your DMs are activated: [how-to-activate-dms-on-discord.md](how-to-activate-dms-on-discord.md "mention")
{% endhint %}

## Start the Purchase

1. Go to our [official discord server](https://discord.gg/323HfHyGW4).
2. Visit the Channel [„Äåüõí„Äçorder](https://discord.com/channels/420677294345158657/883023777259212831/1104473886449479701).
3.  Click on the Order Button.\


    <div align="center">

    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (16).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption><p>Order Button</p></figcaption></figure>

    </div>
4. After you click "**üõí Order**", you should receive a direct message from our bot.
5.  Now choose your product category.\


    <div align="center">

    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (38).png" alt="" width="374"><figcaption><p>Example of the category list</p></figcaption></figure>

    </div>
6.  Now choose your product/s. **Advice:** You can choose multiple products!\


    <div align="center">

    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (18).png" alt="" width="371"><figcaption><p>Example of the product list</p></figcaption></figure>

    </div>
7. By clicking outside the menu you confirm the input.
8.  You are now in the **checkout overview**. Here you can see all the products currently in your shopping cart and their prices.\
    **You now have 4 options:**\

    <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (10).png" alt="" data-size="line"> = **Do you have made a mistake? Start over and reset your checkout.**\


    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (21).png" alt="" width="512"><figcaption><p>Example of the checkout overview</p></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
You can also click on order in the specific product channel to add it to the checkout.

### [<img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/emojis/863177942817308732.webp?size=56&#x26;quality=lossless" alt=":paypal:" data-size="line">](https://www.paypal.com/) Pay with PayPal

11. Click on the PayPal button.
12. Now an embed is shown with the PayPal checkout.
13. Open the Link to PayPal.\


    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (25).png" alt="" width="506"><figcaption><p>Example of the PayPal Checkout Link</p></figcaption></figure>
14. Now you will see a checkout page from PayPal directly, follow the instructions there.\


    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (24).png" alt="" width="344"><figcaption><p>Example PayPal Checkout Screen</p></figcaption></figure>
15. Once the PayPal payment is completed, the product will be added to you automatically.

### [<img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/emojis/863178289874993173.webp?size=56&#x26;quality=lossless" alt=":paysafecard:" data-size="line">](https://www.paysafecard.com/) Pay with PaysafeCard

{% hint style="danger" %}
Due to the **PaysafeCard guidelines** we can only accept **German cards**!!!
{% endhint %}

11. Click on the PaysafeCard button.
12. Now enter the PaysafeCard Code.\


    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (27).png" alt="" width="504"><figcaption><p>Example of the PaysafeCard Embed</p></figcaption></figure>
13. Once you have entered the code you will get a confirmation page.
14. Confirm the PaysafeCard Code.\


    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (28).png" alt="" width="374"><figcaption><p>Example of the PaysafeCard Confirm Menu</p></figcaption></figure>
15. The code has now been sent with the checkout information to **our staff team**. We will now verify the code, after which you will receive your product.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Please note that the verification can take up to 24h!
{% endhint %}

### [<img src="https://cdn.discordapp.com/emojis/1020373583274442812.webp?size=56&#x26;quality=lossless" alt=":stripe:" data-size="line">](https://stripe.com/) Pay with Stripe

11. Click on the Stripe button.
12. Now an embed is shown with the Stripe checkout.
13. Open the Link to Stripe.\


    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (22).png" alt="" width="503"><figcaption><p>Example of the Stripe Embed</p></figcaption></figure>
14. Now you will see a checkout page from Stripe directly, follow the instructions there.\


    <figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (4).png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption><p>Example Stripe Checkout Screen</p></figcaption></figure>
15. Once the Stripe payment is completed, the product will be added to you automatically.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Bank dependent payments may take **3 - 6 business days** depending on the bank. Real time payments like credit card etc. are completed **directly**!
{% endhint %}

## Complete Purchase

16. After the payment has gone through all the processes you will receive a confirmation by DM.
